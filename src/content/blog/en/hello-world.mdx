This is the first post on my blog. I write it in MDX, so I can mix prose with interactive components whenever I need to.

## Why a blog?

I've wanted a space of my own to document what I learn for a while. Twitter is noise, LinkedIn is performative, and GitHub gists get lost. Here I have full control over the format and the pace.

## What you'll find

Notes on the projects I build, lessons I pick up along the way, and occasionally opinions about the ecosystem. Everything bilingual — Spanish and English.
