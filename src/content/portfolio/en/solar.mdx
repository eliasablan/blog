Solar is an interactive gravity and solar system simulator built for the browser. It lets users explore celestial bodies, observe orbital mechanics, control simulation time, and launch asteroids with configurable parameters such as radius, density, speed, azimuth, and elevation.

The project combines a visual 3D experience with control panels for inspecting planets, changing the camera, and experimenting with the gravitational behavior of the system.
